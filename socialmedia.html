The High-Octane Conversion Ad

For this project, I wanted to capture the high-energy environment of 360 Fitness. I chose a high-contrast orange and black palette to create a sense of urgency and power for their anniversary sale. My goal was to ensure that while the "Anniversary Offer" grabs your attention immediately, the functional details remain easy to find. I focused on bold typography and sharp angles to mirror the intensity of a great workout.

Culinary Storytelling Grid

I designed this collage for The Green Kitchen to tell a story that goes beyond the menu. I curated these six images to highlight the harmony between the food, the space, and the people. By using earthy tones and focusing on textures—like the stacked ceramic plates and wooden tables—I aimed to create an inviting, organic feel. For me, this project was about more than just aesthetics; it was about communicating the warmth and "adventure" of a dining experience.
