Illustrations and Graphics

3D Pop‑Out Waterfall

With this piece, I wanted to play with the idea of screen vs. reality, so I imagined what it would feel like if nature literally poured out of my device and into my hands. Letting the waterfall flow straight into the cup makes the scene feel immersive and playful.

Minimal Portrait

This portrait is personal, so I chose a clean vector style to focus on expression rather than details. Using flat colours and simple shapes, I tried to capture the warmth, comfort, and closeness in our faces.

Playful Family Cartoon Scene

Here I wanted to create a fun, chaotic little universe where characters, animals, and objects all share the same playful energy. I used bold colours and exaggerated shapes to show my love for character design.

Giant Duck on the Ocean

The funny contrast between the tiny surfers and the oversized ducklet shows my sense of humour and my interest in surreal compositions that make people pause and smile.

Stretch‑Effect Colourful Houses

By stretching the colours vertically, I turned the scene into a kind of abstract barcode, where each stripe holds a memory of the houses, the sky, and the reflections.
