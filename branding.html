Branding

Sanyojan Luxury Jewelry Campaign

The Vibe: Elegance meets heritage. The Work: I designed this campaign for C. Mansukhlal Jewellers using a royal plum and gold palette to elevate the "Sanyojan" collection. I focused on high-contrast lighting and sophisticated typography to give the brand a premium, "must-visit" feel for their in-store event.

Maa Bhuvaneshwari "Methi Khakhra" Packaging

The Vibe: Fresh, honest, and retail-ready. The Work: For this Khakhra packaging, I balanced traditional snack appeal with modern minimalism. By using clean white space and warm accents, I ensured the nutritional info and "Handmade" USP were the stars, making the product pop on a crowded shelf.

ApnaTurf "Reserve. Play. Dominate. Digital Flyer

The Vibe: High-energy and action-oriented. The Work: I created this dynamic flyer to drive instant bookings. Using a bold "turf green" aesthetic and a curved action mask, I built a visual flow that leads the eye directly from the athlete to the QR code, turning viewers into players.
